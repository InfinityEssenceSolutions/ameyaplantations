Ameya - Vinukonda

Our flagship white sandalwood plantation in Bollapalle, Vinukonda, spans over 82 acres with more than 30,000 sandalwood trees already planted. The plantation is managed by talented entrepreneurs with several decades of experience in real estate, and hospitality sectors. The farm is grown under continous expert guidance from IWST scientists, ensuring optimal growth and prosperity.


We invite you to seize this exceptional investment opportunity by owning quarter-acre units in our plantation. This investment option offers tax-free income and significantly higher returns compared to other alternatives such as stocks, mutual funds, and real estate. Additionally, it includes land appreciation and a hands-off approach where the investment team takes care of everything.

Project Layout

The Layout is encircled by pictresque green hills and the plots are thoughtfully designed to maximize accessibility, convenience, and natural beauty. Roads are accessible from all the plots. Each plot (or unit) measures 0.25 acres and are designed to provide ample space for the growth of 100 sandalwood trees.
